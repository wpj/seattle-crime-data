About

This project is a tool to visualize crime data in the area one mile around CenturyLink Field. You can visualize the data in several ways:

  • Map

    • View all the crime plotted as clustered points on the map. Zoom in and click on a map marker to see a particular crime's information.

    • View a heat map to see which areas have the most crime

  • Charts

    • Pie chart that displays a breakdown of crime that occurs within a mile of CenturyLink Field.
    • Bar graph that displays a breakdown of the types of crime that occur within a mile of CenturyLink Field
